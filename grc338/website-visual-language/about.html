Scott Garrett, a Del Mar local with a profound love for smoothies and a passion for sharing the joy they brought him, founded Scotty's Smoothies in the heart of California's sun-kissed coast. From its inception, Scotty's has been more than just a smoothie shop; it's a beacon of health, happiness, and community in the bustling streets of Del Mar.

The journey began when Scott, a lifelong enthusiast of fresh, wholesome foods, noticed a gap in the market for truly high-quality, nutritious smoothies that didn't compromise on taste. Fueled by his vision, Scott embarked on a quest to source the finest local ingredients, from succulent fruits to verdant greens, all to craft smoothies that were both delicious and nourishing.

In 2021, Scotty's Smoothies opened its doors, quickly becoming a sanctuary for health-conscious individuals and smoothie aficionados alike. What set Scotty's apart was not just the unparalleled quality of its smoothies but the sense of community it fostered. Scott's ethos was simple: bring people together in a space where they could enjoy nature's bounty in a glass, share laughs, and create memories.

Today, Scotty's Smoothies is more than just a local favorite; it's a testament to Scott Garrett's enduring legacy of spreading joy through the simple, yet profound, pleasure of a well-made smoothie. At Scotty's, every sip is a blend of California's finest, a moment of pure bliss, and a step towards a healthier, happier life.
